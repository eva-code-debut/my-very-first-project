# study_planner
> a tiny app conceived by a high school student who wanna make life easier during exams

## User's problems:
Planning your revision's schedule can take some time and be a source of stress. The goal of this application is to simplify students' lives: enter three criteria, and a clear schedule is generated instantly.
 
  * Identification: enter the exam's name
  * Difficulty: rate the difficulty of each exam on a scale from 1 to 10
  * Due date: enter the date you'll pass your exam
  

## How was it built (for now):
100% hand generated code: i coded everything myself from the name to the day you should start studying
